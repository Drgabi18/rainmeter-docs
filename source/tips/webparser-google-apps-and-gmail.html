tip by jackfrost.

If you have a Google Apps account associated with your GMail, and you want to use it in a GMail skin, use the following URL in the WebParser measure:

``` ini URL=https://GMailID%40Domain.com:GMailPassword@mail.google.com/a/Domain.com/feed/atom/ ```

Make sure that the items GMailID, Domain.com, and GMailPassword are replaced with your own credentials.

The "%40" string is the encoded form of "@" to avoid a problem where WebParser improperly formats the URL.
